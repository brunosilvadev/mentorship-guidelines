Mentorship Guidelines

Bruno Silva - last updated September 22, 2022

  • The utmost goal is to share knowledge and grow together
  • The code product is secondary, but should be held to high standard
  • Communication and collaboration are fundamental
  • The commitment is not meant to be a burden, but consistence is required
  • Documentation will be written in English, always
  • Test-driven development should be a goal to be worked towards, not a starting requirement
  • Readability is the biggest factor to weigh in coding decisions
